Homeric Hymn to Demeter

Homeric Hymn to Demeter, translated by Colin Young (2019). All rights reserved. Used in ToposText by kind permission of the translator, encountered at a bus stop on Naxos. Colin Young has a BA (Hons.) and PhD from the University of Western Australia (UWA). He specialised in Greek tragedy, particularly the Oresteia, for his PhD. He has taught at UWA and the Australian National University, and has published his poetry in various venues. This text has 19 tagged references to 9 ancient places.
