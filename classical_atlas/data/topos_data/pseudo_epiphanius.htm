Pseudo-Epiphanius, Notitia Episcopatuum

Pseudo-Epiphanius, Notitia Episcopatuum (Protocol order of Bishoprics) of the Patriarchate of Constantinople, possibly from around 640 CE, supplemented by the Greek provinces of Hierocles, Synecdemus, both Greek texts in the public domain, transliterated/formatted by Brady Kiesling.
