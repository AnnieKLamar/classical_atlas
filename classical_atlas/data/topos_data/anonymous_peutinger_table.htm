The Peutinger Map (or Codex Vindobonensis 324) is a large copy probably produced around 1200 of an earlier map derived from a Roman imperial original of about 300 AD. Place names were extracted by JBK from omnesviae.org then the road sequence was corrected from the monumental work of Dr. Richard J.A. Talbert, published at www.cambridge.org, and checked against high-resolution images at atlantides.org This text has 850 tagged references to 702 ancient places.
