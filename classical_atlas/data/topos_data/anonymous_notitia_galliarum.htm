Anonymous, Notitia Galliarum

Notitia Galliarum, Anonymous Registry of Gallic Provinces, from the edition by Otto Seeck, in Notitia Dignitatum (Berlin 1876) online at Archive.org, a work in the public domain semi-adapted to English by Brady Kiesling and freely available for any legitimate purpose. , This text has 95 tagged references to 92 ancient places.

§ 1  What polities are in the Gallic Provinces:
In the province of Lugdunensis Prima four polities:
- chief city, civitas Lugdunensium.
- civitas Aeduorum.
- civitas Lingonum.
- Castrum Cabillonense.
- Castrum Matisconense.

Event Date: 400 LA
